My research focuses on developing AI-driven wearable systems for healthcare, particularly for vulnerable populations such as individuals who stutter and those affected by substance use disorders. By integrating advanced AI technologies like large language models into wearables, I aim to enhance adaptability, accessibility, and interpretability in health monitoring, diagnostics, and personalized interventions.
